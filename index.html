My UX Skills

Teamwork

In today's world, working with a team is not something out of the ordinary. Being able to work well within a group setting is becoming more and more necessary. It brings to life great ideas and allows the team to create a better product.

Prototyping

With prototyping comes great responsibility. The use of wireframes is the key factor as to what the idea of the site or app is and what it will potentially look like. Prototyping is the framework that drives the coding afterwards, making sure the layout is relevant and empathetic to the user.

Research

Research is one of the main sources of information that your design and the project is based off of. Without research the project is subjective. Research adds factual evidence as to why you are designing a certain way.
